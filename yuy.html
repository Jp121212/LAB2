Question #17

What is the mean of the Ozone column in this dataset? Exclude missing values (coded as NA) from this calculation.

Answer

42.1

hw1 = read.csv('hw1_data.csv')
dub = subset(hw1, !is.na(Ozone), select = Ozone)
apply(dub, 2, mean)
